Vaxine: COVID-19 Vaccine Booking App

React.js, CSS

Vaxine was built out of collective confusion to help individuals in New York schedule their vaccine from the comfort of the mobile device or laptop. Simply select your hospital of choice, find an open appointment, provide your information and you’re scheduled for your vaccine!

Vaxine was built during a two-and-a-half day hackathon sponsored by General Assembly and proudly won 2nd place out of 52 competing developers/UI designers.

Empower: Mental Health App

React Native

This app is geared towards the millions of adults who suffer from ADHD. Around 60% of adults are living with the disorder. I wanted to make an app that anyone can have right in their pockets to remind them to complete these tasks so they can have one less thing to worry about forgetting.
